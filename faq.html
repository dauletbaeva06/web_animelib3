Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is AnimeLIB free to use?

Yes! AnimeLIB is completely free. You can browse anime lists, read reviews, and check out news without any charges.

2. Do I need an account?

No account is needed to explore content. However, in the future, we may add an option to create an account to leave reviews.

3. How often is the content updated?

We update the anime list and news sections weekly to keep up with the latest releases.

4. Can I request an anime?

Yes! Just use our contact page and send your request. We’ll try to add it as soon as possible.

5. Are episodes streamed directly here?

No. AnimeLIB does not host or stream anime. We provide information, summaries, and links to official sources only.
